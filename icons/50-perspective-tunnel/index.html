50 · Perspective Tunnel

A pure-CSS warp flythrough tunnel. Six concentric rings emerge from a vanishing point and zoom toward the viewer in an endless cycle — achieved entirely with staggered width/height expansion from 8% to 135% and matching opacity fade. Zero JavaScript, zero SVG.
