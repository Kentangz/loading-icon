21 · Mesh Gears

An interlocking mechanical processing loader. A larger and smaller gear wheel turn in opposing directions with perfectly calculated angular offsets and synchronized teeth speed.

💡 Punched Core Notice: The hollow core in the center of each gear matches your page surface color. Set the --gears-bg property to exactly match the solid background color behind the loader.
