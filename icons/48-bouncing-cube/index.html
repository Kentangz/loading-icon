48 · Bouncing Cube 3D

A stunning solid 3D isometric cube constructed via six closed panels with ambient depth shading. The cube leaps vertically, rotates 90 degrees in mid-air, and lands on a ground shadow plane with aspect-deforming vertical ground-oriented squishes (no sheared distortion).
