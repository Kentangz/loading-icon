25 · Squishy Ball

A satisfying, high-fidelity physics-driven loader. A sphere drops down, flattens/squashes dynamically upon hitting a solid floor, stretches vertically on liftoff, and casts an inverse-scaling fading shadow beneath.

Size variants

sm · 18px ball
md · 28px ball (default)
lg · 40px ball
xl · 56px ball

primary

dark

fast

Custom overrides

pink · hyper fast (0.7s)
cyan · slow & high (2.2s)

Quick usage

<!-- 1. Import the stylesheet -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="squishy-ball.css" />

<!-- 2. Structure — requires sphere & shadow children -->
<div class="squishy-ball" role="status" aria-label="Loading">
  <div class="squishy-ball__sphere"></div>
  <div class="squishy-ball__shadow"></div>
</div>

<!-- 3. Size + color modifiers -->
<div class="squishy-ball squishy-ball--lg squishy-ball--success">
  <div class="squishy-ball__sphere"></div>
  <div class="squishy-ball__shadow"></div>
</div>
