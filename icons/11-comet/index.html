11 · Comet

A conic-gradient arc masked into a ring — the trail fades from transparent to solid, producing a natural comet-streak effect with zero SVG or extra elements.

Quick usage

<!-- 1. Import the stylesheet -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="comet.css" />

<!-- 2. Single element — no children needed -->
<div class="comet" role="status" aria-label="Loading"></div>

<!-- 3. Size + color modifiers -->
<div class="comet comet--lg comet--success"></div>

<!-- 4. Glow variant (great on dark backgrounds) -->
<div class="comet comet--lg comet--glow comet--primary"></div>

<!-- 5. Counter-clockwise -->
<div class="comet comet--ccw comet--danger"></div>

<!-- 6. Full custom override -->
<div class="comet" style="
  --comet-color:     #e05c97;
  --comet-size:      48px;
  --comet-thickness: 5px;
  --comet-speed:     0.6s;
  --comet-arc:       60%;
"></div>
