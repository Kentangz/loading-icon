49 · Digital Segment

A high-fidelity slanted 7-segment digital display digit. Seven segments are formed with hexagonal miter-joints that interlock seamlessly at corner intersections and cycle numbers 0 to 9 using stepped opacity fades, styled with an authentic 10-degree italic slant.
