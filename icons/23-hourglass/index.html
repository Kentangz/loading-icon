23 · Hourglass

A gravity-driven physical sand-dripping hourglass loader. Sand drains from the top bulb to the bottom bulb through a central stream, and the entire glass flips 180 degrees at the end of each cycle.

Quick usage

<!-- 1. Import the stylesheet -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="hourglass.css" />

<!-- 2. Structure — requires top, stream & bottom children -->
<div class="hourglass" role="status" aria-label="Loading">
  <div class="hourglass__top"></div>
  <div class="hourglass__stream"></div>
  <div class="hourglass__bottom"></div>
</div>

<!-- 3. Size + warning color modifier -->
<div class="hourglass hourglass--lg hourglass--warning">
  <div class="hourglass__top"></div>
  <div class="hourglass__stream"></div>
  <div class="hourglass__bottom"></div>
</div>
