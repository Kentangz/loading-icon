03 · Pulse Ring

An expanding, fading ring — ideal for live-status indicators, online badges, and active alerts.

Size variants

sm · 22px
md · 36px (default)
lg · 56px

Color presets

primary
success
warning
danger
white

Variants & use cases

outline dot
fast · amber
online badge

Modifier classes

pulse-ring--fast
pulse-ring--slow
pulse-ring--soft
pulse-ring--reverse

Quick usage

<link rel="stylesheet" href="pulse-ring.css" />

<!-- Basic -->
<div class="pulse-ring"></div>

<!-- With modifiers -->
<div class="pulse-ring pulse-ring--lg pulse-ring--success"></div>

<!-- Custom speed and scale -->
<div class="pulse-ring" style="--pulse-color:#EF9F27; --pulse-speed:0.8s; --pulse-scale:2.2;"></div>
